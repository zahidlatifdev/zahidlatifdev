## About

I am a full stack and mobile app developer based in Faisalabad, Pakistan, building production software for startups, product teams, and business owners.

Since 2020, I have worked across SaaS platforms, eCommerce systems, mobile apps, AI-assisted workflows, dashboards, payment flows, database-backed APIs, deployment, and long-term product maintenance. I care about practical architecture: clean interfaces, reliable backend contracts, predictable data flow, and products that are maintainable after launch.

I am completing my BS in Computer Science at the University of Agriculture, Faisalabad, expected 2026, while working with clients across Pakistan, the US, and international markets.
